Sinigang na Baboy

Description

Sinigang na Baboy is a sour soup with pork ribs, vegetables, and tamarind-flavored broth. It's hearty, tasty, and perfect for cold weather.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly or pork ribs, cut into chunks
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bundle string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 bundle kangkong (water spinach) leaves and tender stems
  • 2 green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 1 pack tamarind soup mix (sinigang mix)
  • Fish sauce or salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the pork in water with onion and tomatoes until tender.
  2. Add the radish and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the eggplant and string beans, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the sinigang mix and green chili peppers.
  5. Add the kangkong leaves and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Season with fish sauce or salt to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

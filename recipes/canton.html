Pancit Canton

Description

Pancit canton is a Filipino dish of Chinese origin which combines yellow wheat noodles and a variety of meat, seafood, and vegetables, blended with a flavorful mixture of soy and oyster sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams Pancit Canton noodles>
  • 250 grams pork, sliced into thin strips
  • 200 grams shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 medium bell pepper (red or green), sliced into strips
  • 1 small cabbage, shredded
  • 100 grams snow peas, ends trimmed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

Steps

  1. Prepare all the ingredients: slice the pork, peel and devein the shrimp, julienne the carrot, slice the bell pepper, shred the cabbage, and trim the ends of the snow peas. Chop the onion, mince the garlic, and prepare the green onions for garnish.
  2. Add the pork slices and cook until they turn brown and are fully cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Saut√© the garlic until fragrant, then add the chopped onions and cook until translucent.
  4. Add the carrots, bell pepper, and snow peas. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the shredded cabbage and continue to stir-fry until all the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  6. turn the cooked pork and shrimp to the pan with the vegetables. Pour in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and fish sauce. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Add the chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  7. Add the Pancit Canton noodles to the pan, making sure they are evenly distributed. Toss the noodles with the sauce and vegetables until they are well coated and fully cooked. This should take about 3-5 minutes. Season with ground black pepper and salt to taste.
